01· The starting line

Where Canna Planet stands today

A strong retail and loyalty foundation, with clear, untapped room on the digital side.

Strengths to build on

  • High in-store loyalty opt-in (10% off to join)
  • 35–40% of sales already online, above average
  • Email ROI reported at 8,476% via Alpine IQ
  • Trees POS + Alpine IQ stack already in place
  • “A-team” in-store experience as the differentiator

Gaps & opportunities

  • No dedicated SEO partner, ranking ~#4 organically
  • No paid ad channels running yet
  • Heavy reliance on social (real account-ban risk)
  • No welcome sequence for new loyalty members
  • No website pop-up; the “10% off” button skips the opt-in
  • No launch process for new locations
  • No performance baseline, limited bandwidth to track it
  • Retention rate, purchase frequency & AOV go unanalyzed
02· The plan

What this engagement sets out to do

Three objectives, in order: measure first, then build, then own the channels you control.

01

Establish the baseline

Set clear, trackable baselines across SEO, email, social, sell-through and web conversion, so every future dollar is measured against a real starting point.

02

Build long-term partners

Find, vet and manage the right vendors for Email, SEO, paid ads and the website: proven operators, not the ones who will overcharge or underdeliver.

03

Own your channels

Reduce reliance on social by funneling customers into loyalty and email, the audiences you keep even if an account goes down.
